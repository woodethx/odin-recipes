Loaded Baked Potato

Description

I chose Ham & Cheese Sandwich also because I am not a cook and I can write up this recipe myself. It's a wholesome comfort food that is highly customizable

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Any fixin's you want, but here are some examples
    • Ground beef
    • Sour Cream
    • Cheese
    • Chives
    • Butter

Steps

  1. Wrap your potato in tin foil
  2. Put it in the oven for however long
  3. Take it out and open it up when it's soft on the inside
  4. Put in your fixin's and mix it up
  5. Enjoy :)
