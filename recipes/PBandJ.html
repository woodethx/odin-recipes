Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

I chose Peanut Butter and Jelly because I am not a cook and I can write up this recipe myself. It is a delicous comfort food that is always a go-to for me

Ingredients

  • Bread (preferably white)
  • Jelly
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Place 2 pieces of bread on a flat surface
  2. Apply a generous coat of peanut butter to 1 piece of bread
  3. Apply a generous coat of Jelly to the other piece of bread
  4. Put the two piece of bread together (jelly side facing peanut butter side)
  5. Enjoy :)
