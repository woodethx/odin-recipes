Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Description

I chose Ham & Cheese Sandwich also because I am not a cook and I can write up this recipe myself. It's a really easy meal to make for lunch, especially if it's last minute

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Place 2 pieces of bread on a flat surface
  2. Apply your choice of condiments to either/each side
  3. Place cheese on one side, ham on the other
  4. Put pieces of bread together
  5. Enjoy :)
